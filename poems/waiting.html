Waiting

losing patience with waiting
it follows me till I break
begging on my knees
give me the keys
to these chains

they keep me
running in circles
stuck in this pain

looking for a way out
washed up on shore
waiting for help

starving

screaming out all night
holding my breath all day
a smile on my lips
and tears in my eyes

suffocating

Waiting

losing patience with waiting
while I’m dancing with Satan
he follows me till I break
got me begging on my knees
“please give me the keys”
to these chains that keep me
running in circles
going insane
stuck in this pain

looking for a way out
washed up on shore
waiting for help

I lay here
suffocating

does anyone hear me,
screaming out all night?
holding my breath all day
a smile on my lips
as tears salt my food

eating becomes a gift
limited to the days
when he doesn’t stay

I lay here
starving

Starting Over

when will the pounding stop?
begging me to open that door
time and time again
incessantly knocking
no end in sight

each page filled with pain
bleeding out slowly
my tears wet the paper

can I start over?
in someone else’s story
where the sun still shines
even when it rains
to wake up as another
without feeling the pain

to relish in the silence
without a single knock
with no door to open
no peering into the beyond
